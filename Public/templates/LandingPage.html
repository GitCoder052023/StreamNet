Connect Locally with StreamNet

StreamNet is a local network group chatting application designed for seamless communication within organizations. Now enhanced with ChatLLama, it provides a classic chat interface for interacting with your Ollama models.

Key Features

Local Group Chatting

Connect and collaborate with your team on your local network—fast, simple, and reliable.

End-to-End Encryption

Every message is secured with encryption, keeping your local conversations private.

ChatLLama

Enjoy a classic chat interface to interact with your Ollama models for real-time AI assistance.

NEW FEATURE

Introducing ChatLLama

A modern real-time chat experience powered by local Ollama models, bringing intelligent AI-based messaging to your network.

Local AI Integration

Chat with your Ollama models locally without sending sensitive data to external servers.

Real-Time Streaming

Watch responses appear character by character with Socket.io-powered streaming.

Model Selection

Switch between different AI models based on your specific use case requirements.

Advanced Formatting

Enjoy markdown support with syntax highlighting for code and technical discussions.

Streaming Responses

Conversation Management
